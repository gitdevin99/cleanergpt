# Cleanup Clone Art Direction

## Visual Thesis

Build the app like a premium, slightly theatrical utility: midnight glass surfaces, hard electric blue actions, hot red storage pressure, and image-led panels that feel persuasive instead of enterprise.

## Content Plan

1. Onboarding: make the problem feel urgent and visual.
2. Paywall: make the offer feel inevitable and polished.
3. Dashboard: make scanning feel alive and rewarding.
4. Feature modules: each utility gets one strong visual idea, not generic cards.

## Interaction Thesis

1. Onboarding advances with soft scale and fade shifts, plus a glowing interstitial.
2. Scanning surfaces animate through count growth and progress drift.
3. Overlays feel like lifted layers, not separate screens.
